# Don't end with vague CTAs

**Rule:** Never end a post with "what do you think?" or "let me know in the comments." Always close with a concrete fork or specific question.

**Why:** Vague CTAs get vague comments. Concrete CTAs get split-room debates. The pattern is consistent across 100+ shipped posts.

**How to apply:** When drafting the closer, ask: "Could the reader give a one-word answer to this?" If yes, ship. If no, sharpen until yes.
