# Binary closer

**Definition:** Close the post with a fork — two named options the reader has to pick between. Forces a choice instead of leaving them passive.

**When to use:**
- Default for short-form posts (under 250 words).
- When the post takes a clear position and you want comments to split along that fork.

**When NOT to use:**
- Long-form posts that already earned a nuanced ending.
- When the topic is genuinely open-ended and a fake binary would feel manipulative.

**Variations:**
- A/B literal: "Are you the X or the Y?"
- Time-fork: "In 6 months you'll either be doing X or Y."
- Identity-fork: "Builders who do X. Operators who do Y. Pick."
