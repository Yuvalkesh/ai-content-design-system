# Time anchor

**Definition:** Open with a specific time marker — a date, a year, "this morning", "two weeks ago" — to ground the reader in a real moment before the idea lands.

**When to use:**
- Almost always for story-shaped posts.
- When the insight is more credible if it's clearly tied to a recent event.

**When NOT to use:**
- For evergreen frameworks or definitional posts.
- When the date would make the post feel stale within a week.

**Variations:**
- Specific date: "On April 27, 2026..."
- Relative recent: "This morning..."
- Era anchor: "Five years ago this would have taken..."
