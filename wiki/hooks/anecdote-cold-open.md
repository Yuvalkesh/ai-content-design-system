# Anecdote cold open

**Definition:** Open the post with one concrete sentence describing a specific scene or moment — no preamble, no "I want to share..." — drop the reader directly into action.

**When to use:**
- Story-shaped posts (>50% of posts).
- When the scene itself does the work of making the topic feel real.

**When NOT to use:**
- Pure framework or list posts.
- When the scene would take more than two sentences to set up.
