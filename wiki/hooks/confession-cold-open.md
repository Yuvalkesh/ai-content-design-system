# Confession cold open

**Definition:** Open with a vulnerability or unflattering admission about yourself — disarms the reader, signals honesty, makes the rest of the post feel earned.

**When to use:**
- Posts where you're about to make a strong claim and need credibility.
- When the topic is sensitive enough that a softer entry helps.

**When NOT to use:**
- When the confession is performative (readers can tell).
- When you'd reuse the same vulnerability phrase from a recent post.
